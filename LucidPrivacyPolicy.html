Privacy Policy

Lucid — Finance Calculator  |  Last updated: February 19, 2026

The short version: Lucid does not collect, store, or share your personal data. All calculations happen entirely on your device.

Overview

Lucid — Finance Calculator ("Lucid", "we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Do NOT Collect

Lucid does not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Your financial data, loan amounts, income, or salary information
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Crash reports sent to our servers

How the App Works

All financial calculations in Lucid — including mortgage, compound interest, student loan, auto loan, personal loan, savings goal, loan comparison, and retirement projections — are performed locally on your device. No data is transmitted to our servers or any third party.

Pro Features & iCloud Sync

If you purchase Lucid Pro, saved calculations may optionally sync via Apple iCloud. This sync is handled entirely by Apple's iCloud infrastructure under Apple's own privacy policy. We never receive or have access to your iCloud data.

In-app purchases are processed by Apple's App Store under Apple's terms and privacy policy. We do not receive your payment information.

Third-Party Services

Lucid does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking SDKs (such as Google Analytics, Firebase, Facebook SDK, etc.).

Children's Privacy

Lucid is a general-audience application. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Since we collect no data from any users, this app is safe for all ages.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or store any of your data on our servers, there is no server-side data at risk. Data you enter into the app stays on your device and, if you enable iCloud sync, in your personal iCloud account.

⚠️ Disclaimer: Lucid is designed for informational and educational purposes only. The calculations provided are estimates and should not be considered financial, legal, or investment advice. Always consult a qualified financial professional before making financial decisions.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the new policy.
